Aarogya - Exist Happily

Description

  • John Cedrick A. Del Rosario, Chinmay Deshpande & Ankit Mohokar

  • March, 2018

Arogyaa a Sanskrit origin word - meaning overall well-being of mind, body, and spirit. This application digitalises prescription and will enable sharing their health history with new doctors easily. Arogyaa will also provides a platform for doctors to collaborate and share their expert opinions. We presented this app at 14th Annual Pace Pitch Contest 2018. This app also won first prize at Pace Moblie App Design Hackathon 6.0.
